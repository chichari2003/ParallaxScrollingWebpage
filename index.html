GREAT WALL OF CHINA

FACT-1

"Great Wall of China is the world's longest wall and the biggest ancient architecture."

FACT-2

"The Great Wall spans approximately 13,170 miles (21,196 kilometers) and dates back more than 2,300 years, constructed over several dynasties.But, nearly 1/3 of the Great Wall has disappeared without a trace.Many parts of the wall have suffered damage due to natural erosion, human activity, and weather. Restoration efforts have been ongoing for centuries."

FACT-3

"The myth that the Great Wall is visible from space without aid is untrue. Although impressive, it's not easily visible to the naked eye from space."

FACT-4

"Over a million workers, including soldiers, peasants, and prisoners, contributed to building the wall. Some estimates suggest that many lost their lives during construction."

FACT-5

"Beyond its namesake of defense, the wall also served as a means of controlling immigration and emigration, facilitating trade, and enforcing border regulations."

FACT-6

"The height of the wall ranges from about 16 to 26 feet (5 to 8 meters), while its width varies from around 15 to 30 feet (4.5 to 9 meters)."

FACT-7

"It's a major tourist attraction, drawing millions of visitors annually to witness this marvel of ancient engineering.The Great Wall of China was designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1987, recognizing its historical significance and architectural grandeur. "
